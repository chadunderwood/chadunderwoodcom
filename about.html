About Chad

Since he was a child, Chad had a love for anything with buttons and electricity. Problem solving always came easy to him. Put a love for technology and problem solving together and you get a well rounded Technologist Chad is also a Husband and Dad. He and his wife have been married 5 years and recently move back to Nashville, TN. She is an accomplished technologist in her own right. They have 4 boys and a busy house. Chad began his tech journey in 2007. Being mostly self taught in technology, Chad landed a help desk role. Manning the phones and working on anything that was thrown in his direction, molded Chad into a well rounded wealth of knowledge. The help desk role also help him understand the importance of customer service and how to achieve a high level of customer satisfaction. He was able to obtain 100% satisfaction for multiple straight years in a atmosphere where 90% was the goal. He quickly became a go to analyst to help train new hires on phone etiquette and managing metrics in a fast paced environment.
